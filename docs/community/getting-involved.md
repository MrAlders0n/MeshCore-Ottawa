# Get Involved

If you’re interested in playing an active role in the community, we’re always looking for volunteers.  
Reach out to **MrAlders0n** with what you’d like to help with.  

Below are some common roles we have in the community.

---

## Community Outreach
- Spread the word on social media (Reddit, Facebook, TikTok, etc.)
- Put posters up around the community

## Community Support
- Welcome new users in Discord
- Answer questions and help new members get started

## Fabrication Team
- For people who want to participate in custom PCB design

## Knowledge Curators
- Maintain and update wiki content

## Tower Tech
- For people who like to climb and help install or maintain repeaters
